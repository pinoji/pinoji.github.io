Research Topics and Teaching

I am interested in computational methodologies for quantifying complex molecular and biomolecular interactions. My work utilizes ab initio and DFT techniques to characterize fundamental energetic properties. Molecular dynamics simulations of large biological complexes are my central interest at this point.

Our most recent research introduces a novel approach designed to elucidate binding mechanics in enzyme-inhibitor complexes where ligand binding occurs within highly flexible protein environments.

I teach Physical Chemistry classes at Arkansas State University. You can reach me at hkoizumi@astate.edu.
